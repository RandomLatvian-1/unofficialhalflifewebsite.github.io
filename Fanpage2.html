What is Half-Life about?

Half-Life is a game where you play as Gordon Freeman, and experiment corrupts the world and infiltrates it with monsters and eventually a massive alien empire that takes over the world! In the first game you have to escape the science facility and in the second you have to defeat the alien empire. It's super fun and I would 100% recommend! It is a shooter and an action game but it is also a 18+ so make sure your allowed to play it.
